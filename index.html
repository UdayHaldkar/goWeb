About Website

goWeb is an online cloud service that act like your personal assistant by maintining your files and documents. It allows you to convert all your paper work into digital files and documents and make your workplace completely paperless We have created a coustomizable work managment system to handle submission of douments and approval workflow, facility to sign your documents with approval seal.This system is also manageable by email. It allow you to converte your workplace completely paperless and establish official communications thorugh emails,automating procedures for taking a step ahead for saving the environment. By the use of our website the use of paper is eliminated or greatly reduced.This is done by converting douments and other papers into digital form, a process known as digitalization.

Our Aims

Our aim is to create a huge impact on the environment through reducing paper consumption by turning paper documents into digitalised documents and create an easy managable workflow environment for our users. With industry claculations that ofice workers on average use a sheet of paper every 12 minutes,over10,000 sheets of paper in a year,and dispose of up to 100 to 200 pounds of paper annually, there are obvious benefits in considering a paperless office
Reducing the use of paper is an environmentally sustainable,money saving approach.Transitioning to a paperless office saves trees,reduces energy use in manufacture and shipping of paper,eliminates the mailing and delivery of paper documents and reduces the need for paper storage.

FAQs

Do we have full access to our data?

goWeb cannot delete your files,tags or notes permanently.It will not remove or replace your file content.
As an additional safe guard you can test your tools on a temporary file.

Is this website free?

You can test goWeb for 30 days with all features for free.No credit card is requied.You can then upgrade to a paid plan,which will give you the full power to goWeb.
Alternatively,we have a limited free plan.

Do we have to learn how to use goWeb?

You don't have to learn how to use goWeb from scratch.We have prepared ready to use workflows or many common applications.You can use them as it is or build something even more awesome from them.

Will I be able to prioritize my documents using goWeb?

Yes goWeb allows user to set different priority levels for uploaded documents. The priority levels can be set as high,normal and low.
Document which require less attention can be set with low priority during upload.
